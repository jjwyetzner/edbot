Study Resources
  1. Khan Academy: A website containing free video lessons and practice problems for a variety of subjects and standardized tests.
  2. Quizlet: Quizlet is a free learning tool and digital flashcard website. You can use existing study sets or create sets of your own.
  3. StudyStack: Another free flashcard website with thousands of study sets available and the option to create your own.
  4. Grammarly: An AI software that helps you improve your writing and correct errors. A free version and discounted version of Grammarly premium exist for students
  5. Schooltraq: An online digital planner that is available for free that helps students keep track of assignments, test scores, and due dates.
  6. Evernote: A digital note taking service that allows users to create digital notebooks and organize their notes.
